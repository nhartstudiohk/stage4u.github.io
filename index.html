NEWS

As the founder of Stage Edu Net

Dorothy is a registered teacher and social worker with more than 18 years of working experience in related fields. She has been particularly interested in areas that relate to special educational needs (SEN) and inclusion in school or community settings.

She promotes “interest-based intervention” to rebuild confidence in children who will then be capable of looking for their own stage to shine. She is keen on training teachers and parents who are raising children with special needs in Hong Kong.
